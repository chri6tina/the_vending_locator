Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our vending location services

Are all pricing packages one-time payments?

Every package listed on the pricing page is a one-time payment. Each transaction is handled through a third-party card processing service and is charged immediately upon ordering. If you're unsatisfied with the list you receive, please contact our support team for a refund.

How reliable is your data?

When you purchase a list from our vending machine locators, our team of researchers creates a custom list just for you. This ensures that the data on new locations is as up-to-date as possible, minimizing the need for replacement requests.

Is this a subscription or a single payment?

It's a one-time fee. As a vending business owner, you won't incur any additional charges beyond the initial purchase.

What regions do you serve?

We specialize in researching high-traffic locations and large companies for vending machines globally! This encompasses "vending machine locations near me." It's worth noting that the majority of our clients are based in the United States and Canada.

Do the locations you provide already host vending machines?

As part of our research services, we furnish details about a business including its name, address, website (if available), phone number, store rating, review count, category, and tags. If you've selected a package with email research, we also include any available email addresses. It's important to note that this information is sourced from public internet platforms, and we cannot confirm whether a specific business has vending machines or any vending services onsite. To ensure the suitability of a location, we recommend contacting the business directly by visiting the location or placing a call. As far as we know, no vending industry locator service has been able to provide such specific information.

Are the locations you offer suitable for my business?

Considering your vending business and the products you intend to sell is the initial step in our research process. With the category data included in our research, you can assess which potential locations are most likely to align with your business. For instance, if you operate a healthy vending machine, gumball machines, sticker machines, or sell honor boxes, the categories we provide will broadly match your criteria.

How do vending machine locators gather this information?

We collect all data from public databases and through research. While we don't have access to specifics like the current traffic at these locations or the number of machines already in operation, our team examines factors such as satellite images of the building to ensure that the facility can accommodate your vending machines seamlessly.

Can you determine if a specific city/zip code already has numerous vending machines installed?

We conduct research on vending machine locations based on foot traffic and common types of high-traffic locations such as hotels, warehouses, etc. However, we don't cover saturation levels or identify nearby vending machines. You would need to discover this information through your own outreach efforts.

Do you reach out to locations on behalf of vending operators?

No, we provide a meticulously researched list for you to contact directly. Our service streamlines the process by conducting the research and eliminating unsuitable locations for your vending machines. However, with our gold package, we collaborate with you in reaching out to companies. There's no set time limit for achieving the locations, but we assure you dedicated efforts until success.

Do you provide refunds?

Yes, refunds are offered on a case-by-case basis. If you're dissatisfied with the newly delivered location list, please reach out to our support team for assistance. We'll evaluate the order to determine if our quality standards have not been met.
