Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our hot leads service

What makes these "hot" leads?

Our hot leads are pre-qualified businesses that have been researched for vending machine potential. We verify contact information, assess location quality, and score each lead based on success probability. These aren't just random business lists - they're carefully selected opportunities ready for immediate outreach.

How fresh are the leads?

We update our hot leads database daily. All contact information is verified within 48 hours of being added to our system. This ensures you're getting the most current and accurate information available.

What information do I get with each lead?

Each hot lead includes: business name, complete address, verified phone number, email address (when available), business category, employee count, and our success score. You'll also get notes about the location's vending potential and any special considerations.

Do you offer refunds if leads don't convert?

Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with the quality of our hot leads, we'll either replace them with new leads or provide a full refund. We're confident in our lead quality and want you to succeed.

Can I buy leads for specific business types?

Absolutely! Our city and state packages include leads from all major business categories (hotels, office buildings, warehouses, manufacturing, etc.). You can also request custom packages focused on specific business types if you have particular preferences.

How quickly can I start using the leads?

Immediately! All hot leads are delivered instantly after purchase. You'll receive a downloadable file with all the contact information, and you can start calling right away. No waiting period or processing time required.
