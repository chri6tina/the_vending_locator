Frequently Asked Questions

How soon will I receive my location list?

Basic packages are delivered within 5-7 days, while Pro and above packages are delivered within 3-5 business days. We'll send you an email notification when your list is ready.

What if I need more locations than my package includes?

You can purchase additional location lists or upgrade to a higher package. Contact us for custom pricing options tailored to your specific needs.

Are the contact details verified and up-to-date?

Yes, all contact information is verified and regularly updated. We maintain a comprehensive database and conduct regular verification to ensure accuracy.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with the quality of our location lists. Contact us within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.

Do you provide ongoing support after purchase?

Yes, all packages include email support. Pro and above packages include priority support options including phone support during business hours.

Can I customize my location search area?

Yes, you can specify your target area by zip code and we'll focus our research on that specific region. You can also request multiple areas if needed.

What types of businesses do you research?

We research various high-traffic locations including warehouses, offices, schools, hotels, hospitals, gyms, car dealerships, and more. Each location is evaluated for vending potential.

Do you help with the actual placement of machines?

We provide the research and contact information, but the actual outreach and machine placement is handled by you. We focus on finding qualified leads for your business.
