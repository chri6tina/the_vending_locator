What We Do

Our comprehensive research service identifies high-traffic locations in your target area and provides you with verified contact information. We save you countless hours of cold calling and research by delivering curated lists of businesses that are most likely to be interested in hosting vending machines.

Location Research

We identify businesses with high foot traffic and employee counts that would benefit from vending services.

Contact Verification

All contact information is verified and up-to-date, ensuring your outreach efforts aren't wasted.

Traffic Analysis

We analyze foot traffic patterns and business hours to identify the most profitable opportunities.

Types of Locations We Research

Warehouses

Large warehouses with multiple shifts and employees who need quick access to snacks and beverages during breaks.

Office Buildings

Corporate offices with employees who appreciate convenient access to refreshments throughout the workday.

Schools & Universities

Educational institutions with students, faculty, and staff who need quick meal and snack options between classes.

Hotels & Motels

Accommodation facilities where guests appreciate 24/7 access to snacks, beverages, and basic necessities.

Hospitals & Medical Centers

Healthcare facilities with staff working long shifts who need quick access to refreshments and snacks.

Gyms & Fitness Centers

Fitness facilities where members need hydration and post-workout nutrition options.

Car Dealerships

Automotive businesses with customers and staff who appreciate refreshments during service visits and sales processes.

Curated Location List

What We Don't Provide

Outreach Services

We provide the leads, but you handle the actual cold calling and sales outreach to secure locations.

Guaranteed Sales

While we provide qualified leads, we cannot guarantee that every location will agree to host your machines.

Machine Placement

We don't handle the physical placement or maintenance of vending machines at locations.
